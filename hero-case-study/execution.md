# Execution

## Overview
Execution required tight coordination across engineering, SRE, data, and customer‑facing teams. I established a structured operating rhythm to ensure alignment, unblock engineering, and maintain transparency with leadership and customers.

---

## Cross‑Functional Leadership

- Facilitated cross‑team debugging sessions

- Latency dashboards launched with data engineering
