## Product Decision Frameworks

These are the frameworks I use to drive clarity, alignment, and high-quality decisions.

---

## 1. Problem → Signal → Insight → Action
A structured way to move from ambiguity to clarity.

- **Problem:** What is happening?  
- **Signal:** What evidence supports it?  
- **Insight:** What does it mean?  
- **Action:** What should we do?  

---

## 2. Impact vs. Confidence vs. Effort
A prioritization model for sequencing work.

- **Impact:** How much value does this create?  
- **Confidence:** How sure are we?  
- **Effort:** What does it cost?  

---

## 3. Risk Buckets Framework
Used for reliability and platform work.

- **Load Risk**  
- **Latency Risk**  
- **Failure Risk**  
- **Observability Risk**  

---

## 4. Alignment Triangle
A tool for cross-functional clarity.

- **Vision:** Where we’re going  
- **Strategy:** How we get there  
- **Execution:** What we do next
