## Stakeholder Communication Example  
### Weekly Reliability Initiative Update (Sample)

**Audience:** Engineering, SRE, Data, Customer Success, Leadership  
**Format:** Weekly written update

---

## 1. Summary
We reduced P95 latency by an additional 6% this week and completed the rollout of deterministic retry logic to 100% of traffic.

---

## 2. What Shipped
- Deterministic retry logic (full rollout)  
- Queue depth dashboard (v1)  
- Carrier performance metrics (beta)  

---

## 3. Key Risks
- Carrier X showing elevated latency during peak hours  
- Worker pool saturation still observed in region EU-West  

---

## 4. Next Week
- Begin traffic shaping rollout (10% → 25%)  
- Add anomaly detection alerts for carrier latency  
- Complete load test for new routing logic  

---

## 5. Ask / Decisions Needed
- Approval to pause Feature Y for two weeks to free engineering capacity
